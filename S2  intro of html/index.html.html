Nara Chandrababu Naidu

New cm of Ap

TDP chief Chandrababu Naidu, who was sworn in as the new Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh today, will be visiting Tirumala with his family this evening. Tomorrow morning, he will offer prayers at the Tirumala temple. Chief Minister Chandrababu will return to Amaravati by noon tomorrow and will head to the Secretariat in the evening. He will assume his duties as Chief Minister in his chamber on the first block of the Secretariat at 4:41 PM tomorrow (June 13).
